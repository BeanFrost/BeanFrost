    It's Great but What's the Catch?

    Social media usage provides billions with the ability to be connected, however its overusage begins to affect one's identity.

    How is This?

  • Self-Sterotyping from group affiliation
  • Past digital footprint and its conlfict with ones current sense of self
  • Consistenty with online/offline self identity
    •

    What can we do to Combat Negative Affects

  • Educating oneself on media literacy
  • Being aware of biases and logical fallacies,
  • Identifying oneself in their own terms
  • Grounding themselves in said identity when going forward
